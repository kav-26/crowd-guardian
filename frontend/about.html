About Crowd Guardian

Crowd Guardian is an intelligent web application built to help people stay safe by providing real-time insights into crowd density at popular public locations. It empowers users to plan visits smartly and avoid overcrowded areas.

🌍 How the Prototype Works

Crowd Guardian aggregates live crowd data from multiple sources, including community reports and simulated traffic data. This information is visualized through interactive charts and map markers, helping users easily understand crowd patterns throughout the day.

🗺️ Interactive Map

The integrated map displays key locations like temples, parks, malls, and transport hubs. Each place is marked with a color-coded indicator showing the current crowd level. Clicking a marker reveals detailed information, assisting users in making safe and timely travel choices.

📢 Reporting Feature

Users can contribute by submitting real-time reports about crowd levels in their area. This community-driven feature keeps the data accurate and up to date, promoting shared awareness and safety.

📈 Charts and Trends

Interactive line and pie charts showcase hourly and daily crowd trends. Users can identify peak hours, plan visits during less busy times, and avoid unnecessary waiting or risks.

🛡️ Safety Tips

  • Avoid visiting places during high crowd periods whenever possible.
  • Maintain social distancing and follow all local safety guidelines.
  • Use the reporting feature responsibly to assist others.
  • Always check live map data and trend charts before stepping out.
